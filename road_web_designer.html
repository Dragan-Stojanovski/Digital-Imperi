Road to sucesfull WebDesigner

29.June.2022

Nowadays, having a website is not a luxury but a necessity that you must not miss. On an annual level, Google searches 1.2 trillion times if you do not have a website, that is 1.2 trillion missed chances. By creating a website for your business that is modern, useful, and optimized a lot of all these people who search on google become your potential customers.


What is Web Design?

Website design is the process of gathering information, market research, creative design, and innovative ideas that are enjoyable for site visitors. You need to give your customers a unique design that will be visually pleasing to them and be user-friendly and easy to use if it is too complicated and they can not cope your customers will not come back to your site again. There are hundreds of ways to turn that design into a website (coding (HTML, CSS, JavaScript Python, PHP, MySql) or with pre-built platforms such as WordPress, Drupal, Wix, and Magento,).


How to become a web designer

1. Learn and improve necessary skills

To get the title of web designer you need to know the technical aspects of building a website. You have several ways to get the necessary skills one of them is to complete a technical faculty. Many web designers especially nowadays when the need for web designers The market is huge are self-taught. All you need is the internet and a desire to learn. The main necessary skills are:


-Good sense for visual design

A good sense of visual beauty is knowing how to choose the right design color typography images for your site so that it will be more comfortable to use and visually beautiful is very close to the user experience.


-UX(User Experience)

User experience(UX) is best explained by how the user feels all the time while using our website. UX is one of the most important factors besides the UI (User Interface) on how our users will like it and whether they will come back again. in UX design to work by thinking like you are the customer and what you would like to see as a customer to be satisfied with the service.


-UI(User Interface)

UI(User Interface) is a design process where designers build software or computerized devices by giving focus on looks and style. The main goal of UI designers is to design something easy and pleasurable to use. User interfaces are access points where the user interacts with design and there are three of them: Graphical user interface(GUIs) Voice-controlled interfaces(VUIs) Gesture-based interfaces To create great and likable Uis(User Interfaces)you should follow the next tips: -Users don't care about the design of our website they care about getting the task done with minimum effort. -User Interfaces(UIs) must be enjoyable you should try to predict what users expect from your site and give it to them in the simplest way that you can find at the moment. -(Uis) should be emotional and get close to your users, Once your users feel comfortable using your website that will associate good feelings between them and your brand.


UI(User Interface) vs UserExperience(UX)

Often confused with each other (UI) design is focused on the surface of the website and the overall feeling that our users will get during the visit. While (UX) design covers the spectrum of user experience on our site.

“Interfaces get in the way. I don’t want to focus my energies on an interface. I want to focus on the job.” — Don Norman, Grand old man of UX design



Programming languages

A good web designer must have a basic knowledge of some programming languages ​​because the best designer is the one who has knowledge of programming and is aware of what can be done and what can not be done in code. One of the basic programming languages ​​that should be known is HTML CSS, PHP, JQuery, and JavaScript. Those who will work independently will have to have more knowledge of programming languages ​​while those who work in agencies or companies will be able to work with basic knowledge because their team will have experienced programmers who will implement their design.


Search engine optimization

Nowadays one of the most important things for a website is to rank high on Google so that more users can reach it but nowadays this is not an easy task because the competition is huge so you will need a lot of knowledge and experience which is irreplaceable.


2. Web Design Tools To Learn

-Adobe XD

Adobe XD is used more by web designers because it allows the site to be given life with some minor limitations so that the client to whom you will present the design will have a better picture of what the website will look like.

-WordPress

You can not go wrong with WordPress, which is the main platform of the Internet, 38% of the web pages attached to the Internet are made on it. It may seem difficult and frustrating at first. But WordPress is easier to use and understand than other platforms or to build with programming language in code.

-Visual Studio Code

Visual Studi Code is free to use modern code editor with many upgrades that will make typing easier for you.
