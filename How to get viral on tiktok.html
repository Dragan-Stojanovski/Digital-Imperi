How to go viral on TikTok in 2022(9 Proven ways to go viral)

10.July.2022

TikTok is the social network that has grown tremendously during the Covid-19 crisis and everyone can't stop talking about TikTok in 2022.

In 2022, TikTok has evolved and changed. It is no longer a social network where teenagers sing and dance. It has turned into a platform that contains all kinds of content, from a full day of eating up to a day in the life of a doctor or astronaut.

It is for these reasons that TikTok achieved its success. Any type of content can succeed on this social network, while on Facebook and Twitter you are quite limited in your creativity.

TikTok monitors every piece of content carefully and gives every clip a chance by showing it to its users, even if you don't have any followers, your video will be seen by many users. If users like it, be ready for your account to go viral, that's why your content must be quality.

High-quality content is your ticket to virality.
If your video is liked by this small group of users to whom TikTok will show it, then it will spread it all over the platform and you are viral, this happens 2 to 3 hours after you post the video, so only after a few hours, you will know if your video is viral or not.



We're going to give you some tips on how to get a viral video on TikTok.

1. Stunning intro to your video!

Life on TikTok is fast and dynamic so you have to attract its users do not let them pass your video to watch other content. So put the most attractive part at the beginning to attract the attention of users to stop to watch your video.


2. Right Video length!

Keep your videos short and to the point. TikTok rates videos by what percentage of the video has been viewed so it's much more likely that someone will view 8 seconds of a 10-second video than 48 seconds of a 1-minute video.


3. Record yourself

With the help of comments and hashtags, TikTok tries to show the right content to the right users who will be interested in that content. In addition to comments and hashtags, you can help TikTok show your video to the right audience by recording yourself. with the help of your phone or a microphone, during which he will receive many keywords that will help him present you to the right audience.


4. Use music that is popular at the time.

On TikTok users feed on trends so you have to provide them with content that will keep up with the current trend. You can use your music but you can always add popular background music to your videos quietly this strategy has proven to be profitable for many TikTok videos to go viral.



5. Share quick tips and tricks.

On TikTok, in addition to boring dances that are constantly repeated, you can also find educational content and advice that can make your everyday life easier. When creating educational content or sharing tips, make sure the videos are short and clear so that the user gets the answer they need in a few seconds and doesn't waste time listening to unnecessary information.



6. Elicit comments from your audience.

People like to express their views and opinions so use this to your advantage by provoking your followers to open a debate and comment on your videos, this will give TikTok a reason to rank your videos higher. displays to multiple users.



7. Incorporate a little controversy into your content.

A little controversy never hurt anyone. For example, if people are divided you take a stand on one side this will cause those on the other side to come to your comment section and write all kinds of opinions but you are not interested in that you are only interested in it that more comments on the video will rank you higher on TikTok. People love controversy and many celebrities abuse it to increase their popularity or media to get more readership why don't you use this too?



8. Reply To All Comments You Received

Understand that the TikTok algorithm works so that the videos that have the most comments continue to be shown to more users, which is a key factor for your video to become viral. It is in your interest to have as many comments as possible, it works especially well if with your comments you get users to keep commenting to spread the debate. But don't abuse this by commenting too fast, hold back or you may get banned for 24 hours from commenting that is not in your interest, be smart with your comments and it will pay off.



9. Post often

Every new post of yours is a chance for you to go viral, TikTok is played on numbers, the more you post, the more chances you have of being noticed. With frequent posting, you become more experienced, so that each subsequent post will be better and of higher quality, and with that, greater chances. to go viral.


Start something and stick with it most TikTok users need more than a few months to find the right niche that suits them best. Once you find yourself start recording quality content and most important don't give up it's only a matter of time before one of your videos goes viral and paves your way to success on TikTok.
