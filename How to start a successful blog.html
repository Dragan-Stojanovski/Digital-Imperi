How to start a successful blog?

09.July.2022

If you want to share your views on the world, skills, and knowledge with the whole world while earning money then blogging on the Internet is the perfect place for you.

Sharing your views and opinions has never been easier than it is today when you can do it online and have an audience from all over the world. YouTube is a good platform where you can share your opinion in video format with others. But if you are shy and you want to keep your anonymity then blogging on the internet is made for you. Some bloggers are blogging because they want to share their opinion with others and some blog to earn money. Also, you can combine these two to share your opinions on the internet and get paid for that.


What is a blog?

The blog differs from other large complex sites in that the blog is a more personal format where one or several people write and share their knowledge on a specific topic that is precisely specified, for example, food, video games, fitness, football, etc. Unlike official informational portals that must be public, you write on your blog how you want in the format you like with infinite freedom in your expression.



How to start blogging in 2022 and is it worth it.

Many of you are wondering if and how much you can earn from a blog in 2022. Writing a blog is one of the best ways to make money online because you have endless freedom in your expression. Maximum flexibility because it doesn't take much time and you can work in whatever part of the day you want and from wherever you want, all you need is a laptop and an internet connection.

Professional bloggers in the world earn millions annually. But you don't have to be a professional, you can make blogging a side job that you will be doing during the weekend or in your spare time and you will provide yourself with additional income, but if you want to be a professional and to earn a serious amount from blogging then you will have to invest time, effort and a lot of energy.


How much do bloggers earn?

Some bloggers earn over $200,000 a year and work only 20 to 40 hours a week, which is the main advantage of the blog. But as a beginner, after only 10 months you can start earning from 500 to 1000 dollars a month, and after a year the earnings become more serious. you are committed.

One of the main conditions is that your blog is in English so that it will be attractive to the whole world which will bring you significantly more visitors. The main thing is to let your blog mature and you will be very satisfied with the earnings.

Then it is important to choose an area that is paid not all niches are paid the same!

Choose right topic

The first thing you need to do is to choose a topic on which you will write and have high expertise on that topic so that your blogs are relevant to visitors. The biggest mistake of most bloggers is that they choose a topic that earns the best but they have no knowledge of it after they soon realize that because their content is of poor quality, their blog is losing visitors.


The key to a good topic is to have high expertise in it, if your readers notice that you do not have needed expertise in a certain topic then reading your blogs will be a waste of their time.


The right name for your blog

Although some underestimate the value that a good name gives to your blog. First and foremost you need to find a name that describes what information your users will find on your blog. Also, think about how your name will fit in your URL address, and social networks because once you start branding your domain it's hard to change everything.


When choosing a name for your blog, it must contain a term that explains your business, as well as being easy to pronounce and remember.


Create a website

To begin with blogging first reserve a domain and hosting for your website where it will be attached to the Internet. Our recommendation is to create your website in WordPress, which is free and easy to use with effortless plugins for easy management of your website.


For more information on using WordPress if you are a beginner, you can refer to our Beginner's WordPress guide.

If you are creating it yourself, take the time to familiarize yourself with the platform on which you will create your site, and if you hire a developer, make sure that he will be there for you if there are certain problems with the site.


Guide to your blog content.

After you have created your site on which you will blog and upload it to the Internet. Now it remains to enter content that will give value to your site. Without quality content, your site does not offer any value to your users. There are basic rules when creating content for your blog. Perhaps the most important thing in creating content for your site is to be SEO (Search Engine Optimization) optimized, for that you will have to familiarize yourself at least with the basics of SEO or hire a professional agency or an SEO consultant who will help with the optimization of your blog.

You can learn the basics of SEO in our 5 key factors for a better SEO.


Fundamentally, you must extract a keyword through which your users will come to your website.



Your content must contain the basic elements of a blog post:

  • Headline-The first thing that will attract users.
  • Introduction to the contents-Familiarizes users with what to expect.
  • Subheadings-Divide the text into wholes and further entice users to continue reading.
  • Photos-Photos are easier to follow than text and will capture more easily what a particular piece of text is about.
  • Links-Link your texts if they are of a similar theme.
