In this guide, we'll dive deeper into how AdSense works and whether it's worth monetizing your work with AdSense in 2022.


Introducing Google Adsense

Adsense is a program that allows you to display ads on your YouTube channel, blog, or social networks and earn money when someone clicks on one of the ads displayed on your media.

Adsense is one of the most popular methods of earning for new bloggers and YouTube channels because it is very simple to use, you just need to upload a link to your site and ads will automatically be displayed in the places you specify for that purpose.

The way it works is simple, many companies pay for Google to advertise them, by applying for Google AdSense you agree to be a partner of Google and permit it to advertise the companies that have paid for advertising on your website, and you as a reward for allowing Google advertises on your website or YouTube channel and you get paid for every click on the ads.


Pros and Cons of Making Money With Google Adsense


Google Adsense has many positive advantages, we will list a few:

  • Signing up for Google Adsense is free, you won't pay a single cent to start displaying ads and making money.
  • You will be approved quite easily, the conditions are not difficult so new blogs can also be approved.
  • One of the biggest advantages is that you have the opportunity to determine on which part of your page the ads will be displayed. Whether in the beginning, in the side part, or at the bottom, it's up to you to choose.
  • Google will pay you at the end of each month to be paid you need to have earned a minimum of $100 from ads if you have not met $100 for the month you will wait until you have earned $100 and you will be paid.
  • In addition to your blog, you can also monetize a YouTube channel, but you need to meet the condition of having a minimum of 1000 subscribers and having 4000 hours of views on your videos.
  • From one Google account you can display ads on many websites or YouTube channels, you don't need to register a new Google Adsense account for each channel.

Like everything in life where there are positive advantages, there must be a negative side, we will list some of the negatives of Google Adsense.


  • Follow Google's rules if you try to cheat it will ban you instantly.
  • You need to have an audience if you want to make money like in any field in a way your visitors are your potential customers they come to click on the ads and you earn money for every click if you don't have visitors to click you can't make money. The key to making money is to have a lot of traffic on your site.
  • You must have an audience if you want to earn from Google Adsense, as in any business you must have a community. If you do not have traffic on your site, you will not have clicks on the ads displayed by Google Adsense and you will not be able to earn.

Despite all the positives and negatives, AdSense is a great way to make money nowadays, but you should be aware that making money from Google AdSense takes time and patience.


Types of Google AdSense ads

Text ads are just sentences and come in different shapes and sizes.

Then there are advertisements in graphic form, usually in the form of an image with a certain text on it. Advertisements also come in the form of video and audio, but these forms are less common.


How Google AdSense Pays You

Google will pay you at the end of each month via direct deposit or check. To be paid, you must have earned a minimum of $100 for that month. If you have not earned $100 the month you have earned $65, you will not be paid for that month, but that $65 will be transferred to you for the next month.

On your Google AdSense account, you will have total analytics on how much you have earned and how much you will be paid.


Some useful tips for making money with Adsense

Read Google's rules carefully and don't break them.

Don't click on ads on your page, Google is smart and doesn't want its rules to be broken, and one of them is that you can't click on ads on your own or that some of your loved ones click, follow this rule, don't lie to yourself. that you will deceive Google.

Optimize your website for mobile phones. In 2022 70% of internet visitors will come from a mobile phone so make sure your website is optimized to be displayed on a mobile device as well.

Displaying quality content is a key factor to successfully earning through AdSense, by offering quality content you will in turn get visitors who can click on your ads.

Be honest use a good honest marketing strategy first and foremost SEO optimization and article marketing which means quality content on your site.


Conclusion

It is obvious that there are many ways to make money online, whether through AdSense or affiliate marketing, but the key to making money is to have traffic, if you don't have traffic, everything falls into the water.

AdSense is great for earning from your blog or YouTube channel for both beginners and advanced but you should be aware when you are a beginner that you should not hope for big earnings at the beginning the earnings will be somewhat minimal but give it time to grow.
